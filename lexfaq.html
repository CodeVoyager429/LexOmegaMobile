❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Platform Use

  • Q: What is LexOmega?
    A: LexOmega is a legal-tech platform offering tools for legal research, case planning, document generation, and secure client interaction.
  • Q: Do I need a subscription?
    A: LexOmega offers free access with optional premium upgrades. No card required for trial use.
  • Q: Can I submit cases?
    A: Yes. Cases can be uploaded through the Dashboard or Documents section.

Security

  • Q: Is my data secure?
    A: Yes, LexOmega uses military-grade encryption and session-based security layers.
  • Q: Who can access my files?
    A: Only you, unless explicitly shared or uploaded to public trial boards.

Getting Help

  • Q: How do I get support?
    A: Use the contact form on the Support page or email us directly at support@echoversellcio.com.
  • Q: Is live chat available?
    A: Yes. Click the 💬 Chat link in the navbar.
